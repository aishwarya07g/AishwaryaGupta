About

Hello there!

I am Aishwarya, a computer science major based in Bangalore, India with a thriving passion to change the perspective of how technology and design work hand-in-hand, in a cognitive way. I am also an artist, a travel enthusiast, a space fanatic, a dog lover and a crazy indie music fangirl.

As a child, I always wondered about space. Yes, I was indeed trapped in its enchanting beauty, the glowing stars and its magnificent planets. Ever since my childhood, I've been privileged to grow up around people who inspire me to achieve my dreams. My sister, who has always been fascinated with the field of science and art, enthralled me to get creative and ambitious with technology.

Today, I am a final year student who would love to explore the world full of opportunities and wishes to fulfill her childhood dreams. As Randy Pausch once said, 'Follow your passions, believe in karma, and you won't have to chase your dreams, they will come to you.'

When I'm not testing my brain, I'm either traveling places

Or trying out new delicacies and desserts

Or crossing things off my Bucket list!
